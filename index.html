What is ULTANGBER?

An Innovative Mathematics Learning Game Kit

ULTANGBER is a specially designed game kit that encourages students to solve mathematics problems while playing. The ULTANGBER game method incorporates thinking skills and edutainment elements to create a meaningful and enjoyable learning environment.

Teachers can utilise ULTANGBER as a learning aid for reinforcement and enrichment activities in mathematical problem-solving skills. Students can also use the kit as a recreational activity with peers and family while fostering interest in mathematics.

  • Based on the classic Snakes and Ladders concept
  • Integrates Form 1‚Äì3 Mathematics DSKP curriculum
  • Features 3 levels of question difficulty
  • Power Cards for strategic gameplay elements

Komponen & Cara Bermain

Papan Cabaran ULTANGBER

Papan permainan yang direka khas dengan petak-petak istimewa seperti ular, tangga, integer positif/negatif, dan petak beku.

Kad Cabaran

Kad soalan mengikut aras kesukaran (Aras 1-3) yang sepadan dengan kurikulum DSKP Matematik Tingkatan 1-3.

Kad Kuasa

4 jenis kad istimewa yang memberi kelebihan strategik seperti membekukan lawan, menggandakan langkah, atau menukar tempat.

Token & Dadu

Token berwarna untuk pemain dan dadu untuk menentukan jumlah langkah dalam setiap giliran.

Cara Bermain ULTANGBER

1

Gerak Token

Pemain baling dadu secara bergilir-gilir. Bilangan yang diperoleh menunjukkan jumlah langkah yang token akan digerakkan.

2

Jawab Cabaran

Apabila token mendarat di petak istimewa (ular, tangga, integer, atau freeze), pemain perlu mengambil Kad Cabaran dan menjawab soalan matematik.

3

Menang Strategi

Pemain yang berjaya menjawab soalan aras 3 akan mendapat Kad Kuasa untuk strategi permainan yang lebih menarik.

Petak Istimewa dalam ULTANGBER

Tangga

Pemain ambil Kad Cabaran. Jika jawab betul, naik tangga ke petak paling atas. Jika salah, kekal di bawah tangga.

Ular

Pemain ambil Kad Cabaran. Jika jawab betul, kekal di kepala ular. Jika salah, token turun ke hujung ekor ular.

Integer Positif

Pemain ambil Kad Cabaran. Jika jawab betul, bergerak ke hadapan berdasarkan nilai positif. Jika salah, kekal di petak.

Integer Negatif

Pemain ambil Kad Cabaran. Jika jawab betul, kekal di petak. Jika salah, undur ke belakang berdasarkan nilai negatif.

Freeze

Pemain yang tiba di petak ini akan dibekukan selama 1 giliran. Pemain hanya boleh bermain semula pada giliran berikutnya.

Components & How to Play

ULTANGBER Challenge Board

A specially designed game board with special squares including snakes, ladders, positive/negative integers, and freeze squares.

Challenge Cards

Question cards by difficulty level (Level 1‚Äì3) aligned with the Form 1‚Äì3 Mathematics DSKP curriculum.

Power Cards

4 types of special cards that grant strategic advantages such as freezing opponents, doubling moves, or swapping positions.

Tokens & Dice

Colour-coded player tokens and a die to determine the number of moves on each turn.

How to Play ULTANGBER

1

Move Your Token

Players take turns rolling the die. The number rolled determines how many squares the player's token moves forward.

2

Answer the Challenge

When a token lands on a special square (snake, ladder, integer, or freeze), the player draws a Challenge Card and must answer the mathematics question.

3

Win with Strategy

Players who successfully answer a Level 3 question earn a Power Card to add exciting strategic elements to the game.

Special Squares in ULTANGBER

Ladder

Draw a Challenge Card. If correct, climb the ladder to the top square. If wrong, stay at the bottom of the ladder.

Snake

Draw a Challenge Card. If correct, stay at the snake's head. If wrong, your token slides down to the snake's tail.

Positive Integer

Draw a Challenge Card. If correct, move forward based on the positive value. If wrong, stay on the square.

Negative Integer

Draw a Challenge Card. If correct, stay on the square. If wrong, move back based on the negative value.

Freeze

A player who lands on this square is frozen for 1 turn and may only play again on the following turn.
