Apa Itu ULTANGBER? What is ULTANGBER?

ULTANGBER (Ular Tangga Berhalangan) ialah kit pembelajaran gamifikasi yang mengubah latihan tradisional menjadi pengalaman bermain yang interaktif dan menyeronokkan.

ULTANGBER (Challenge Snakes & Ladders) is a gamification learning kit that transforms traditional practice into an interactive and enjoyable gaming experience.

Diilhamkan oleh penyelidikan pendidikan yang menunjukkan bahawa penyertaan aktif dan interaksi dalam bilik darjah bukan sahaja meningkatkan hasil pelajar tetapi juga mengurangkan kebimbangan dan tekanan.

Inspired by educational research showing that active participation and interaction in the classroom not only improves student outcomes but also reduces anxiety and stress.

⚠️

Masalah Problem

Kira-kira 2/3 pelajar kekal pasif semasa sesi latihan walaupun memahami bahan, kerana kebimbangan membuat kesilapan di hadapan rakan dan sifat monoton latihan berulang.

Approximately 2/3 of students remain passive during practice sessions despite understanding the material, due to anxiety about making mistakes publicly and the monotonous nature of repetitive exercises.

✓

Penyelesaian Solution

ULTANGBER mengurangkan kebimbangan sosial dan meningkatkan motivasi melalui pembelajaran berasaskan permainan, menjadikan sesi latihan lebih menarik dan berkesan.

ULTANGBER reduces social anxiety and increases motivation through game-based learning, making practice sessions more engaging and effective.

  • 01 Dua Format Pelengkap — Kit Fizikal untuk pembelajaran kolaboratif, Versi Web untuk latihan individu. Two Complementary Formats — Physical Kit for collaborative learning, Web Version for independent practice.
  • 02 Mudah Disesuaikan — Boleh digunakan untuk pelbagai subjek dan tahap kesukaran. Easily Customizable — Can be used for various subjects and difficulty levels.
  • 03 Kos Rendah — Hanya RM 25 untuk menghasilkan satu kit fizikal lengkap. Low Cost — Only RM 25 to produce one complete physical kit.

Komponen ULTANGBER Kit ULTANGBER Kit Components

01

Papan Cabaran Challenge Board

Papan permainan 5×8 (40 petak) dengan 3 jenis petak istimewa: Tangga (peneguhan positif), Ular (titik cabaran), dan petak Cabaran (pencetus soalan). 5×8 game board (40 squares) with 3 types of special squares: Ladders (positive reinforcement), Snakes (challenge points), and Challenge squares (question triggers).

02

Kad Cabaran Challenge Cards

30 kad soalan merentas 3 aras kesukaran (Mudah, Sederhana, Sukar) untuk pelbagai peringkat pembelajaran. 30 question cards across 3 difficulty levels (Easy, Medium, Hard) for various learning stages.

03

Kad Kuasa Power Cards

12 Kad Kuasa (3 jenis × 4 salinan) menambah kedalaman strategik dan unsur tidak dapat diramal kepada permainan. 12 Power Cards (3 types × 4 copies) add strategic depth and unpredictability to the game.

04

Komponen Sokongan Supporting Components

Termasuk Helaian Jawapan, dadu, token pemain, dan Buku Panduan lengkap untuk pengalaman permainan yang sempurna. Includes Answer Sheets, dice, player tokens, and comprehensive Guidebook for the complete gaming experience.

Keberkesanan ULTANGBER ULTANGBER Effectiveness

Ujian rintis dengan pelajar sekolah menengah menunjukkan hasil yang menggalakkan dalam pelbagai aspek pembelajaran. Pilot testing with secondary school students showed promising results across multiple learning aspects.

👥

😊

Pelajar setuju, sangat setuju atau neutral bahawa mereka menikmati permainan Students agree, strongly agree, or neutral that they enjoyed the game

💡
Nota Penyelidikan Research Note

Ujian ini dijalankan dalam skala kecil dan hanya memberi tumpuan kepada persepsi, bukan hasil akademik. Ujian lanjut diperlukan untuk menguji kesan inovasi terhadap hasil akademik pelajar dalam jangka panjang. Testing was conducted on a small scale and focused only on perceptions, not academic outcomes. Further testing is required to assess the innovation's impact on students' academic outcomes in the long term.
